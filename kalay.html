About University

Technological University (TU), Kalay, is a technological university located in the Sagaing Region of Myanmar. It was founded in 1999 and has evolved from a technical institute to its current status as a university under the Ministry of Science and Technology.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Kalay) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    1999: Established as a Government Technical Institute.
    2001: Upgraded to a Government Technological College.
    2007: Upgraded to its current status as a technological university.
