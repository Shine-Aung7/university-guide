About University

Technological University (TU), Sagaing is a public university in the Sagaing Region of Myanmar, offering undergraduate engineering degrees. It has evolved from an initial technical school to its current university status.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Sagaing) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    1990: Established as a Technical High School on September 17.
    1998: Upgraded to a Government Technical Institute (GTI) on December 1.
    2007: Became a Government Technological College (GTC) on January 20.
    2012: Achieved its current university status on February 23.
