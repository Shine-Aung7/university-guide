About University

Polytechnic University (Lashio) is a public higher education institution in Myanmar. It's located in Lashio, a major city in northern Shan State. The university is a polytechnic, which means it focuses on applied sciences, engineering, and technology. It was formed by merging two older institutions: the Technological University (Lashio) and the University of Computer Studies (Lashio).

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Lashio) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    The university's history is a story of gradual upgrades from technical school to a university. The lineage of its engineering side began as a Government Technical Institute (GTI) in 1999. It was then upgraded to a Government Technical College (GTC) in 2001, and finally received its university status as the Technological University (Lashio) on January 20, 2007.
