About University

Computer University(Mandalay)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(မန္တလေး))is located near Taoh Village in Sintkaing Township, Kyaukse District, Mandalay Region. It is (2.6) Miles for from Yangon-Mandalay High Way Road. The total area of the university is (143.18) acres.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Mandalay) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    1999: It was established as the Government Technical Institute (GTI) on November 11.
    2009: It was upgraded to the Government Technological College (GTC) on April 24.
    2010: It officially became Technological University (Yamethin) on April 22, falling under the administration of the Ministry of Science and Technology.
