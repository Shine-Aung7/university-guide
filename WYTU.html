About University

West Yangon Technological University (ရန်ကုန်အနောက်ပိုင်း နည်းပညာတက္ကသိုလ်) is a public technology university, located in Hlaingthaya, Yangon, Myanmar.
WYTU (West Yangon Technological University) is the third-best engineering university in Myanmar after YTU (Yangon Technological University) and MTU (Mandalay Technological University).
After the YTU building closed due to the 8888 Uprising, the second campus was established in Hlaing Thar Yar in 1996. As a result, some people even perceive WYTU (West Yangon Technological University) as a second campus of YTU (Yangon Technological University).
This move was an important step in continuing education, providing better facilities and a more stable learning environment for students.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to West Yangon Technological University is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    West Yangon Technological University was established successfully on 15 December 2005, by the key responsible persons of Ministry of Science and Technology.
    But now it is under the Ministry of Education and Ministry of Science and Technology in 2021 again.
    West Yangon Technological University is situated nearly the vicinity from Kampyo village, Hlaingthaya Township in Yangon Region and then its campus areas was 220.32 acres.
    In July 2015, it was certified ISO 9001:2008 by Bureau Veritas of France. On 15 December 2015, it passed its 10-year anniversary as a university.
