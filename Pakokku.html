About University

Technological University (TU), Pakokku is a public university located in the Magway Region of Myanmar, offering engineering education at the undergraduate level.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Pakokku) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    December 27, 1999: Opened as the Government Technological Institute (GTI).
    October 20, 2002: Upgraded to the Government Technological College (GTC).
    January 20, 2007: Achieved its current university status as Technological University (TU), Pakokku.
