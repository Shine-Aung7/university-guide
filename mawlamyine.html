About University

The Technological University (TU), Mawlamyine, was established on December 15, 1982. It is a technological and engineering university located in Mawlamyine, the capital of Mon State, Myanmar. The university focuses on producing globally competitive engineers.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Mawlamyine) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    1982: Established as a Government Technical Institute.
    1999: Upgraded to a Government Technological College.
    1998: Upgraded to a Government Technological Institute.
    2007: Achieved its current status as a Technological University.
