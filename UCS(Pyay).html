About University

The University of Computer Studies, Pyay (ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(ပြည်)), is a university in Pyay, Bago Region, Myanmar, offering courses in computer science and information technology.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to University of Computer Studies, Pyay is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government.

    History

    University of Computer Studies, Pyay is a government funded university located in Pyay, Bago Region with an emphasis is on computer engineering at the undergraduate and graduate levels.
    Founded in 2004 as a Government Computer College (GCC) and during the first year of GCC only computer application trainings were offered.
    Starting from 2005, undergraduate student admissions have begun.
    In 2007, Government Computer College (Pyay) became a university named Computer University (Pyay).
    Its name was changed to University of Computer Studies (Pyay) in the year 2017.
    The campus has an area of 17.68 acres and lies to the south of 081/2 milestone on the highway from Pyay to Aunglan.
