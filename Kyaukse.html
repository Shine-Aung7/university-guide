About University

Technological University Kyaukse (KTU) is a public university located in Kyaukse, Myanmar, offering undergraduate engineering education. It evolved from a Government Technical Institute established in 1998, reaching university status in 2007. The institution is known for its focus on providing vocationally and professionally oriented science and technology programs.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Kyaukse) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    1998: Established as a Government Technical Institute.
    2001: Reformed as a Government Technological College.
    2007: Upgraded to the status of a Technological University.
    2021: Became operational under the supervision of the Ministry of Science and Technology.
