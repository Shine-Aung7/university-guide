About University

Computer University(Myitkyina)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(မြစ်ကြီးနား))is situated in No. 21/23 between ZawJun and Taryoe street in the Shansu (south) quarter, in Myitkyina, capital city of Kachin State in Myanmar. On 20 January 2007, GCC (Myitkyina) was promoted to the Computer University (Myitkyina).

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Government Technological College(Myi) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    University of Computer Studies(Myitkyina) was promoted and opened as Government Computer College (Myitkyina) from 2000-2001 Academic year. It has been upgraded to Computer University (Myitkyina) since January, 2007 and human resource development activities are being carried out continuously and energetically.
