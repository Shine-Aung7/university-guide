About University

Computer University(Meikhtila)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(မိတ္ထီလာ))is located in Meiktila (sometimes called Meikhtila), Mandalay Division, Myanmar. It has been producing the graduates in computer sciences and technology majors called CS and CT.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Meikhtila) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    In October 2001, UCS-Mtla was established as government Computer College (GCC). In January 2007, it has been promoted from GCC to University of Computer Studies (Meiktila).
