About University

The University of Computer Studies, Mandalay (UCSM) (မန္တလေး ကွန်ပျူတာ တက္ကသိုလ်), located in Mandalay, is a Myanmar IT and computer science university.
UCSM offers bachelor's, master's and doctoral degree programs in computer science and technology.
The majority of its student body is from Upper Myanmar.
Administered by the Ministry of Education, UCSM is the official university for all the Government Computer Colleges in Upper Myanmar, whose students may continue their advanced studies at UCSM.
UCSM is also a Cisco networking academy.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to University of Computer Studies, Mandalay is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government.

    History

    UCSM was established in May 1997 and held its first classes in September 1997.
    Under Myanmar's system of specialized universities, UCSM was the first specialized university in Upper Myanmar for computer science and technology.
