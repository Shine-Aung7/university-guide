About University

Computer University(Kyaingtong)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(ကျိုင်းတုံ)) is situated in Yan Hla Village, Kha Pha quarter, Kyaing Tong township, East of Shan State. It is 4 miles far from Kyaing Tong and 33.11 acres wide.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Kyaingtong) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    University of Computer Studies (Kyaing Tong) was founded as Government Computer College(GCC) on 19 March 2001. In 20 January 2007, GCC(Kayaing Tong) was upgraded to University level.
