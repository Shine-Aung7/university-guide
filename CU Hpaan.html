About University

Computer University(Hpa-an)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(ဘားအံ)) is located on the west bank of Salween River near the city of Hpa-an Township, Kayin State in Myanmar. The university is administered by the Ministry of Science and Technology (Myanmar).

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Hpa-an) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    Computer University, Hpa-An was formerly opened as Government Computer College (GCC) on 3 September 2001. The university was then promoted to university level on 20 January 2007.
