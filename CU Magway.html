About University

Computer University(Magway)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(မကွေး)) was located in Ayemyathaya Village, and was moved to a new campus near Magyikan Village on the Magway-Taungdwingyi Road in the 2012–2013 academic year. The university, administered by the Ministry of Education, offers undergraduate and master degree programs in computer science and technology.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Magway) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    History University of Computer Studies (Magway) was founded as Government Computer College, adjoining to the office of Union Solidarity and Development Association, Magway Division on 4 September 2000. And then, it was transferred to Ayemyathayar Village, Nankakyun Track, North-East of Myathalun Pagoda on 6 October 2001.
