About University

Technological University, Myitkyina (နည်းပညာတက္ကသိုလ် (မြစ်ကြီးနား)) is located in Kachin State, Myitkyina City, Myanmar.
On 1 September 1997, the high school was upgraded to the Government Technical Institute (G.T.I) level and then on 2 October 1999, it became the Government Technological Colleges (G.T.C) level.
On 20 January 2007, it was again upgraded from College to University. On 28 April 2006, transfer to Swe Aike Quarter, Panmtee village and open the new Technological University with the three stories building.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Myitkyina) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government.

    It is started enhancing as Technical Institute in 1st September, 1997 and Government Technological College in 2nd October, 1999.
