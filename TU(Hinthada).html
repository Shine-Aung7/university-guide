    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Hinthada) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government.

    History

    The Technological University (Hinthada) in Myanmar started as Technical High School (T.H.S) on August 1, 1977.
    It was located in the south part of Tar Ngar Sge Quarter, Hinthada Township, Hinthada District, Ayeyarwady Division.
    It was again up to Government Technological College (G.T.C).
    The College was moved to the present University on April 21, 2003.
    During the academic year of 2015/2016, the total numbers of students attending at TU (Hinthata) are 1,500.
    According to the current classroom situations, there are enough rooms for the total numbers of students.
