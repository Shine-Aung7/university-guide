About University

Polytechnic University (Kyaingtong) is a higher education institution in Myanmar, located in Kyaingtong, Shan State. It was established as a polytechnic university on November 21, 2024, by merging two pre-existing universities: the Technological University (Kyaingtong) and the University of Computer Studies (Kyaingtong). This merger was part of a government initiative to consolidate technical education and provide a more hands-on, practical learning environment for students.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission requirements for these universities are generally based on the Myanmar matriculation exam (the national high school final exam). For engineering programs, applicants typically need a minimum score in English, Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. For computer science programs, a minimum overall score on the matriculation exam is required.
