About University

Computer University(Pathein)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(ပုသိမ်)) is a public university located in Pathein Township, Ayeyarwady Region, Myanmar. It offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in computer science and related fields.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Pathein) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    History Pathein University came into existence by being upgraded to Pathein Intermediate College (7-7-1958), Pathein College (2-11-1964), Pathein Degree College (30-11-1981) and Pathein University (2-9-1996) by stages. It is Located in Yayaka (3), Kanthonesint quarter.
