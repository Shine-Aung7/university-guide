About University

Technological University Kyaukse (KTU) is a public university located in Kyaukse, Myanmar, offering undergraduate engineering education. It evolved from a Government Technical Institute established in 1998, reaching university status in 2007. The institution is known for its focus on providing vocationally and professionally oriented science and technology programs.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Loikaw) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    TU (Loikaw) was initially established as Government Technical High School on 30 July 1982.
    It was promoted to Government Technical Institute on 1 December 1998. It was upgraded to Government Technological College on 10 September 2001.
    It was promoted to Technological University on 20 January 2007.
