About University

Technological University, Magway (နည်းပညာတက္ကသိုလ် (မကွေး); also known as TU (Magway)) is a university located near the village of Kanbyar near Magway in the Magway Region of Myanmar.
The area of the university is 28.82 acres.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Magway) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government.

    History

    The university was founded on 27 December 1999 as Government Technological College, Magway (GTC Magway), offering two-year diplomas in vocational studies and three-year Bachelor of Technology (B-Tech) programs.
    On 20 January 2007, it became a Technological University offering undergraduate degree programs in Civil Engineering, Electrical Communication Engineering, Electrical Power Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronic Engineering, and Chemical Engineering.
