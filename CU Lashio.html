About University

Computer University(Lashio)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(လားရှိုး)) is located on the Mandalay-Lasho Union Road, 7 miles away from Lashio. width 112.22 acres

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Lashio) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    The day the Government Computer College was inaugurated- 4.9.2000. The day it was opened as a computer university- 20.1.2007.
