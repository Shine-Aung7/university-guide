About University

Polytechnic University (Dawei) is a higher education institution in Myanmar, located in Dawei, Tanintharyi Region. It's a polytechnic university, which means it focuses on applied sciences, technology, and engineering.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Polytechnic University (Dawei) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    1998: It became a Government Technical Institute (GTI).
    1999: It was upgraded to a Government Technological College (GTC).
    2007: The Technological Colleage became Technological University (Dawei), offering bachelor's degrees in engineering.
    2024: The Technological University Polytechnic University (Dawei).
