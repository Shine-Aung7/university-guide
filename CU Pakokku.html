About University

Computer University(Pakokku)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(ပခုက္ကူ)) is a public undergraduate university located in Pakokku, Magway Region, Myanmar. Students study various computer disciplines, including hardware, networking, programming, imaging, and artificial intelligence. Its uniform is white for upper wear and light blue for longyi.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Pakokku) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    History Government Computer College (Pakokku) was established on 21 January 2002. It became Computer University (Pakokku) on 20 January 2007 and was later renamed University of Computer Studies (Pakokku).
