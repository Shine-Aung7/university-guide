About University

Government Technological College(Myingyan)(Burmese: နည်းပညာကောလိပ်(မြင်းခြံ)) is a technical college located in the Myingyan District of Mandalay Region, Myanmar. Also known as the Government Technological College (Myingyan), it is an institution providing technical education and skills development in various fields.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Government Technological College(Myingyan) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    The Government Institute of Technology (Myingyan) was inaugurated on March 11, 2014.
