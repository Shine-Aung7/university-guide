About University

Computer University(Myeik)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(မြိတ်)) is a university in Myeik, Taninthayi Region, Myanmar, offering courses in computer science and information technology. The total area is 25.71 acres (10.40 ha).

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Myeik) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    History Government Computer College (Myeik) was opened in the National Solidarity and Development Association Hall in 4 September 2000. In 20 January 2007, it was promoted into University Level. So, it has become University of Computer Studies (Myeik). In 15 May 2012, it was transferred into Shwe-Du Village.
