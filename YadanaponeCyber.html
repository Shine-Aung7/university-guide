About University

Yadanabon Cyber City is Myanmar's largest information technology (ICT) park, located near Pyin Oo Lwin. Established in 2007, it houses a technology university and various local and foreign tech companies.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Yadanabon Cyber City is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    2010: The University of Technology (Yatanarpon Cyber City), also known as UTYCC, was founded on October 17, 2010.
    July 2012: An e-learning center was established with assistance from the Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA).
    Since 2012, online course content has been developed annually.
    The university maintains a double master's degree program with the University of Miyazaki, Japan.
    UTYCC provides internship programs and collaborates with industry partners and projects, including the Mekong Skills to Work USAID Project.
