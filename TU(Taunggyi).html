About University

Technological University, Taunggyi is situated in Ayetharyar township, Taunggyi District, Shan State, Myanmar.
The university is run by Ministry of Education (Myanmar).
It was established in 1992 as Government Technical Institute and was upgraded to Technical College in 1997 and finally it was upgraded to University level in 2007 and it is known as Technological University, Taunggyi.
The school is 84.482 acres in area.
About 500 students graduate from this school annually.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Taunggyi) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government.
