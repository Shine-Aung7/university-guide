About University

Computer University(Taunggyi)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(တောင်ကြီး)) is situated in the Southern Shan State, Myanmar.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Taunggyi) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematicsare eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    The Government Computer College (Taunggyi) was established on August 15 in 2000, which was upgraded to University of Computer Studies (Taunggyi)on 20th January 2007
