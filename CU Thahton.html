About University

University of Computer Studies (Thaton) was established in 2000 and is now located at the fort of the Wondami Hilllock in God village, Thaton Township, Mon State with the aim of nurturing IT professionals.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Thahton) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    The Computer University (Thaton) is situated at the fort of the Wondami Hilllock in God village of Thaton Township, Mon State. At first, Government Computer College (Mawlamyine) was opened on 4th September 2000 in the compound of Government Technical College(Mawlamyine) in Mon State. It was later moved next to Mawlamyine Industrial Zone in Naung Pin Zate Village Tract of Kyaikmayaw Township. In 20th January 2007, it was upgraded to the University level and named as the Computer University (Mawlamyine). At present, the University is so named as the Computer University (Thaton) as it was moved to Thaton in 20th November (2011). The Grand Opening Ceremony of the Computer University (Thaton) was successfully held on 5th December 2011.
