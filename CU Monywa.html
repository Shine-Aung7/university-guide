About University

Computer University(Monywa)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(မုံရွာ))is a public university in Monywa, Sagaing Region, north-western Myanmar. The Ministry of Science and Technology-run university offers bachelor's, and graduate degree programs in computer science, and computer technology. Students may continue advanced studies at the University of Computer Studies, Yangon in Yangon.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Monywa) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    1999: It was established as the Government Technical Institute (GTI) on November 11.
    2009: It was upgraded to the Government Technological College (GTC) on April 24.
    2010: It officially became Technological University (Yamethin) on April 22, falling under the administration of the Ministry of Science and Technology.
