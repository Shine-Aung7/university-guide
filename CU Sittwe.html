About University

Computer University(Sittwe)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(စစ်တွေ)) is located in Pyitawthar quarter, Sittwe, Rakhine State, Myanmar. It was formerly opened as Government Computer College on 1 October 2001, and upgraded to university level on 20 January 2007. Professor Dr. Zaw Tun (PhD (IT)) is the principal of the UCSS. In 2018, there were over 300 students at the university.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Sittwe) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    Government Computer Collage (Sittway) was initially opened on 1st September, 2000. GCC(Sittway) was on Union Street, Pyay Taw Thar Quarter, Sittway, Rakhine State, Myanmar. The board of the government of Union of Myanmar upgraded it to the University of Computer Studies (Sittway) on 20th January, 2007.
