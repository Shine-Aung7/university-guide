About University

Technological University, Meiktila (နည်းပညာတက္ကသိုလ် (မိတ္ထီလာ)) is located on the right side of Meiktila-Pindale Road in Meiktila, Mandalay Region.
Its area is 51.65 acres.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Mandalay) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government.

    History

    The University was transferred to the present site in 2008. The former University was in Tawma Village-tract east of Meiktila.
    It was originally formed on 1 December 1986 as Government Technical Institute (Meiktila) in Tawma Village near Meiktila.
    It became Government Technological College on 25 November 1999. The University was inaugurated on 20 January 2007 at that place
