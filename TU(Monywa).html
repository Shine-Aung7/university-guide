About University

The Technological University, Monywa (နည်းပညာတက္ကသိုလ် (မုံရွာ)) is located on a 48.209-acre campus in Minywa, Sagaing Division, in northwestern Myanmar.
It was established in 1982 as Technological High School.
In 1986 it became Government Technological Institute.
In 1999 it was promoted to Government Technological College (GTC).
In 2007 the GTC was upgraded to university status.
Now "Technological University (Monywa)" offers both undergraduate and graduate programmes.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Monywa) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government.

    History

    The Institute of Technology (Mumbai) was inaugurated as the Government Industrial High School on July 30, 1982. It was opened on November 10, 1999, as well as to the Technical University on January 20, 2007.
    During the ongoing Myanmar civil war, the university was involved when protests in Monywa were met with a deadly police crackdown.
