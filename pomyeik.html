About University

Polytechnic University (Banmaw) is a higher education institution in Myanmar. It was established by combining the former Technological University (Banmaw) and University of Computer Studies (Banmaw).

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Polytechnic University (Myeik) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    Technological University (Banmaw): This university began as a Government Technical College (GTC), established on December 1, 2002. It was subsequently upgraded to a Technological University on January 20, 2007, offering Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) programs. It officially became Polytechnic University (Banmaw) on 2024, falling under the administration of the Ministry of Science and Technology.
