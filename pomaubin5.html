About University

Polytechnic University (Maubin) is a higher education institution in Myanmar, located in Maubin, Ayeyarwady Region. It was formed by the merger of the Technological University (Maubin) and the University of Computer Studies (Maubin). This consolidation was part of a nationwide initiative to restructure and enhance technical education.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Polytechnic University (Maubin) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government
