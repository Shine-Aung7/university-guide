About University

Technological University (Hmawbi), founded in 1989, is a public university in the Yangon Region of Myanmar. It evolved from a Technical High School to its current status as a university in 2007. The university is ISO 9001:2015 certified and its engineering programs are accredited by the Myanmar Engineering Council.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Hmawbi) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    1989: Established as a Technical High School.
    1998: Upgraded to a Government Technical Institute (GTI).
    2007: Upgraded to Technological University (Hmawbi).
    2015: Awarded ISO 9001:2008 certificate, later upgraded to ISO 9001:2015 in 2018.
