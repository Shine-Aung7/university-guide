About University

Technological University (Hpa-An) is a public university located in Hpa-an, the capital of Kayin State in Myanmar. It was established in 1993 and falls under the purview of the Ministry of Science and Technology.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Hpa-An) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    1993: Founded as the Government Technical High School (Hpa-an).
    1998: Upgraded to a Government Technological Institute.
    2000: Upgraded again to a Government Technological College.
    2007: Achieved its current university status as Technological University (Hpa-an).
