    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Government Technological College(Mohnyin) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    The Government Technical Institute (Mohnyin) established on January 1, 2004. Then in 2007, on January 20th, it was extended and opened as the Government Technological College (Mohnyin). Then in 2007, on January 20th, it was extended and opened as the Government Technological College (Mohnyin). A.G.T.I courses have been opened since the 2013-2014 academic year to this day. Bachelor of Engineering (BE) graduates were last produced for the 2017-2018 academic year. Short-term courses are also offered annually according to local needs. In 2022, it was renamed as the Government Technical Institute(Mohnyin).
