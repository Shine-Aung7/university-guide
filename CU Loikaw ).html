About University

Computer University(Loikaw)(Burmese: ကွန်ပျူတာတက္ကသိုလ်(လွိုင်ကော်))is a university in Loikaw, Kayah State, Burma. It is near the Loikaw Airport.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Computer University(Loikaw) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government

    History

    It was founded as the Government Computer College on February 21, 2001, and it became a university on January 20, 2007.
