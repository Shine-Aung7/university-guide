About University

Polytechnic University (Pinlon) offers undergraduate programs in engineering and computer science, similar to other polytechnic universities in Myanmar. The curriculum emphasizes a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing students for the workforce. The university's vision is to produce graduates who can contribute to the nation's technological and economic development.

    • Admission / Permission

    Admission to Technological University (Yamethin) is based on the results of the Myanmar University Entrance Examination (also known as the Matriculation Examination). Students who have passed this exam with a minimum total score in subjects like English, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics are eligible to apply. The specific required marks can vary each academic year and are determined by the university and the government
